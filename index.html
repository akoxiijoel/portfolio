Questions

Before you ask.

How fast is the turnaround?
Script within days of our call; the finished video typically lands inside 1–2 weeks depending on complexity. Rush timelines are possible — ask.
Motion design or talking head — which do I need?
If trust in a person sells your offer (coaches, founder brands), talking head. If the product itself is the star (AI, SaaS, apps), motion design. On our call I'll tell you which — and why — before you spend anything.
What do you need from me?
One call and access to whatever exists: screen recordings, product demos, phone footage, or just you talking. Rough is fine. The script and the edit do the heavy lifting.
Can I run the video as an ad?
Yes — that's the point. Every video is delivered ready for both organic posting and paid placement, in the formats each platform wants.
What about revisions?
You approve the script before anything is animated, and one revision round on the edit is included — so surprises don't survive to delivery.
